Mohandas karamchand Gandhi(2 October 1869-30 january 1948),commonly known as Bapu,was an indian lawyer,anti-colonial nationalist and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for india's independence form British rule,and to later inspire movements for civil rights and freedom accross the world.The honorific prefix mahatma(Sanskrit:"great-souled","venerable"),first applied to him in1914 in south Africa,is now used throughout the world.
